How to get a Personal Loan in India

Personal Loan is an amount you borrow from a Financial Institution for uses like funding a marriage, making a downpayment, paying bills, settling credit card dues etc. It can be considered as a short amount ranging from Rs 20,000 to Rs 1 lakh which can be repaid in tenure ranging from 3 months to 1 year. Now let's look at Personal Loans from Peer to Peer Lending perspective, which is new in India.

Peer to Peer Lending in India

The Reserve Bank of India regulated the P2P Lending Industry in 2017 by releasing a set of guidelines on October 4th 2017. Since then multiple companies have taken up the NBFC-P2P license to start Peer to Peer lending operations in India. Cashkumar is one such company which has been around since 2016 and has disbursed over 4000 loans till date worth over Rs 17 crores.

Types of Personal Loans

A Personal Loan falls into many categories other than buying a house (Home Loan) or a vehile (Auto Loan/Two-Wheeler loan) or funding a business (Business Loan). People in India get Personal Loans to fund marriages, to travel abroad and for tourism, to consolidate their credit card debts, to improve or renovate their homes or pay medical bills which are not covered by insurance.

Places where you can get a Personal Loan in India

Personal loan in India can be availed across the country via banks and NBFCs. Peer to Peer Lending platforms or NBFC-P2P have a presence throughout the country as well bringing varied choices to the Indian borrower. Cashkumar offers Personal Loan via P2P Lending in India across 9 cities. These cities include Bangalore, Chennai, Mumbai, Delhi NCR, Pune, Hyderabad, Ahmedabad, Coimbatore and Visakhapatnam
